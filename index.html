Some words about me and a recent CV

why “flexible framework”? A flexible framework is a programming nirvana. The application structure and coding patterns are just right. Its a balance between getting the design flexible enough to meet as-yet unknown extensions but rigid and framework-y enough to meet the current challenge. The concept really applies beyond programming: you don’t want to over-engineer but you want something nice to use and performant.

why “.xys”? The extension (.xyz) is because it is multi-dimensional (geddit, cartesian co-ordinate axes) and conveniently also one of the cheapest extensions available!
